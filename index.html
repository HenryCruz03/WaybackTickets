Miami,FL

May 18th 1968 - The Miami Pop Festival

Experience the nicknamed "Granddaddy of Woodstock"

the Miami Pop Festival made history as one of the first major rock festivals, featuring legends like Jimi Hendrix, The Mothers of Invention, and Blue Cheer. With thousands gathered under the Florida sun, it was a psychedelic explosion of sound and culture, setting the stage for future music festivals.

Relive David Bowie's Longest Show

David Bowie played his longest show ever at The Chili Pepper, a 36-song, three-and-a-half-hour epic, and now, with time travel technology, you can relive the magic firsthand.

March 9th 1980 - Prince at Sunrise Musical Theater

The Beginning of Prince's Career

In 1980, at Sunrise Musical Theatre Prince took the stage for his Fire It Up Tour, marking his first South Florida performance and offering a glimpse of the greatness to come.
